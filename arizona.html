Arizona Trip Itinerary

Week 1: Sedona

Monday to Friday (Evenings)

  • Monday Evening: Arrival and Sunset at Airport Mesa: Arrive in Sedona and head to Airport Mesa for one of the best sunset views, setting the tone for your visit.
  • Tuesday Evening: Stargazing Tour: Book a stargazing tour to experience Sedona's clear, star-filled skies, a perfect evening activity in this International Dark Sky community.
  • Wednesday Evening: Tlaquepaque Arts & Shopping Village: Spend your evening exploring this arts and shopping village, designed in the style of a traditional Mexican village, and enjoy dinner at one of its restaurants.
  • Thursday Evening: Red Rock State Park: Enjoy a tranquil evening hike in Red Rock State Park, or simply relax amidst the scenic views.
  • Friday Evening: Downtown Sedona: Wander through the downtown area, checking out the galleries, shops, and perhaps ending your day with a relaxed dinner with views of the red rocks.

Saturday

  • Explore Vortex Sites: Dedicate your day to visiting the energy vortexes of Sedona, such as Bell Rock, Cathedral Rock, and Boynton Canyon. Consider joining a guided tour for deeper insight.

Sunday

  • Leisure in Sedona: Take a more laid-back approach with a jeep tour of the red rocks, a visit to Slide Rock State Park, or by revisiting any favorite spots. Close out with a nice dinner at a top local restaurant.

Week 2: Scottsdale

Monday to Friday (Evenings)

  • Monday Evening: Old Town Scottsdale: Begin your week with an evening stroll through Old Town Scottsdale, visiting art galleries and dining at a local restaurant.
  • Tuesday Evening: Scottsdale Fashion Square: Spend some time at one of the largest shopping centers in the Southwest, complete with a variety of dining options.
  • Wednesday Evening: Scottsdale Waterfront: Enjoy the public art and ambiance of the Scottsdale Waterfront area, a great place for a relaxing evening walk.
  • Thursday Evening: Scottsdale ArtWalk: Participate in the Thursday evening ArtWalk in downtown Scottsdale, exploring various galleries and enjoying the lively atmosphere.
  • Friday Evening: Desert Dining Experience: Have dinner at a restaurant like Elements at Sanctuary Camelback Mountain, where you can dine under the stars with stunning desert views.
